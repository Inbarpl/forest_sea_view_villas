Ano Kariotes · Samothraki

A secluded forest, watching the sea

Forest Sea View Villas are located in the village of Ano Kariotes, within a lush and private forest plot with open views over the Aegean. A short ride — or a leisurely 20-minute walk — brings you down to the sea, or a little further to Therma.

Both houses are fully equipped with a modern kitchen, Starlink WiFi, and everything you need. A short walk through the village leads to a beautiful hidden stream with natural pools.
